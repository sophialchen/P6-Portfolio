About Me

My name is Sophia Chen and I am a senior studying Computer Science at Northwestern University. Something interesting about me is that I actually switched into the computer science major right before starting my senior year. I had always felt like there was something missing in my previous majors, and after taking a few computer science courses, I found out it is something I really enjoy. I like how computer science is not only logically challenging, but also has a lot of room to be innovative and express yourself creatively.

In my free time, I enjoy circus fitness and doting on my dog, Petey.
